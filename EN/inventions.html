Inventions and Proposals

Module for Construction Columns and Prefabricated Construction Column

A prefabricated construction column consists of several stacked modules. This simplifies the structure and manufacturing technology, reducing cost and making the connection between modules more reliable and less labor-intensive.
Author: Mykhailo Horskyi

Patented


Wind Generator

The goal of this invention is to create a simple wind turbine construction without a wind direction tracking mechanism, while providing protection against precipitation and maximizing airflow power efficiency.
Author: Mykhailo Horskyi

Patented


Subsoil Capillary Irrigation System for Plants

This system is designed for farming, gardening, and parks. It includes moisture dispensers and rupture detectors, providing efficient and even irrigation by delivering water underground.
Author: Oleksandr Tovmach

Power Supply Device

The goal of this device is to improve ease of use while ensuring consumer and maintenance personnel safety from high-voltage failures during battery charging.
Author: Mykhailo Horskyi

Shock Absorber

This invention belongs to the field of instrument engineering, specifically to suspension systems for highly dynamic moving objects, protecting equipment from vibrations and shock loads.
Author: Mykhailo Horskyi

Method for Treating the Human Musculoskeletal System

This model belongs to medicine, specifically to rehabilitation medicine, and can be used for general improvement and treatment of musculoskeletal disorders in both home and clinical environments.

Handle for Dental Instrument

The goal of this invention is to improve the handle’s grip and comfort, reducing strain on the doctor's fingers while using the instrument.
